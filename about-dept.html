About the Department

Vision

To be a leading department in computer science and engineering education and research.

Mission

The mission of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering is:

  • To provide quality education in computer science and engineering.
  • To foster research and innovation in emerging areas of computer science.
  • To prepare students for successful careers in academia and industry.
  • To promote ethical practices and lifelong learning among students and faculty.

Facilities

The department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, including:

  • Computer Labs with high-performance workstations.
  • Research Labs for AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science.
  • Networking and Cybersecurity Labs.
  • Software Development and Testing Labs.
